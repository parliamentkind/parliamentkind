How to fix Poor Man's Shield and get it back to the game?"

For balance, we will change the assembly of the item

Poor Man's Shield

The item was removed from Dota 2, because of the imbalance that it brought, at a small cost it gave a lot of impact.

The whole problem lies in the assembly of this item. It includes Slips of Agility, which give agility, and with it, armor, attack speed and damage.

However, we can replace them with Ring of Protection, which give armor. Let's compare the 2 assemblies in the table below.

+ Slips of Agility more powerful + Ring of Protection
too much impact > just more armor
makes a melee character much more stronger than a rangers > still, just more armor for comfortable line stage
